About Us

We Are Professional Cleaning Service Company.

Imagine coming home after a long day at work to find that all your housework has been done. The floors are sparkling, the bathroom is gleaming, and all the beds have been made. For 5 years, our team of cleaning professionals has been providing comprehensive house cleaning services in Hanoi and all over the country.

 

Vision

An Sinh strives to become one of the 10 largest industrial cleaning service providers and project operators in Vietnam.


Mission

Committed to providing the community with quality, environmentally friendly services with the creativity and enthusiasm of An Sinh people, is the driving force for the next generations.


Core Values

Professional

  • Work in accordance with procedures and regulations.
  • Communicate politely and courteously with colleagues and partners.
  • Neat and agile style according to standards.

Solidarity

  • Putting the interests of the collective above the interests of the individual.
  • Recognizing and encouraging personal strengths.
  • Share knowledge, personal experience with all colleagues on issues in work and life.

Eager to learn

  • Proposing many options when solving work.
  • Effectively apply knowledge learned in daily work.
  • Be open and ready to receive appropriate expertise and technology.

Responsibilities

  • Be responsible for yourself and your work.
  • Responsible to the community and society.

Honest

  • Always be honest, straightforward, transparent at work.
  • Consistency and consistency in thought and action.
